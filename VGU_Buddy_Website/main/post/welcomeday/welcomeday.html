Welcome Day

September 10, 2025

Join us for an exciting introduction to university life at VGU, where new students discover their academic journey and connect with the vibrant campus community.

Welcome Day at Vietnamese-German University marks the beginning of an extraordinary academic adventure. As new students step onto our beautiful campus, they are greeted with a carefully orchestrated celebration that introduces them to the unique blend of Vietnamese warmth and German precision that defines our educational approach.

The day begins with an inspiring opening ceremony featuring welcome speeches from university leadership, faculty presentations, and performances by our talented student organizations. New students receive their orientation materials, campus maps, and connect with their academic advisors who will guide them throughout their studies.

Interactive campus tours showcase our state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories, collaborative learning spaces, the comprehensive library, and recreational areas. Students explore different faculties, meet professors, and learn about the diverse academic programs, research opportunities, and international exchange possibilities that await them.

The celebration continues with cultural activities, networking sessions, and a festive lunch where students begin forming friendships that will last throughout their university years.
